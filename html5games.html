Magmar.io

Magmar.io is to put it simply, an Agar.io clone. It's suprising that we're this upfront about it. Avoid enemies and viruses, increase your cell's mass by consuming agar and other players and create stratagies in this seemingly slow yet action packed game.


Hextris.io

Hextris.io puts a spin (pun intended) on the Tetris we all know and love. Stop the blocks from leaving the inside of the outer gray hexagon and keep your combo in this addictive timepass.


Elevator Saga

This game is for the programming-inclined of you and is a bit of a brain@!#? but trust me if you spend some time doing it you'll love it. Your task is to program a bunch of elevators using Javascript in the most efficient way possible. Depending on how well you can do it, you can go through even more difficult stages.


Clumsy Bird

Another ripoff! Who knew? I think this one is a little easier to discern, but in case you can't figure it out, it's Flappy Bird. Fly the bird through the gaps in the pipes and don't touch anything. Try and get the highest score you possibly can in this frustrating yet extremely addictive game.


A Dark Room

You wake up to pitch black and frigid cold. Do what you need to do in order to survive in this thrilling text-based adventure that will leave you on the edge of your seat at every twist. This game does not work with adblock on.
